About


The Smart Tracker (TRK) is an alarm and localization system that may be used for tracking your company material goods. The system uses two main types of components in order to keep an eye on your goods. The main devices that are doing the monitoring are called parents and children. The main difference between the two of them is the fact that a parent contains a GSM and a GPS module that allow him to report it's position. The children's scope is to communicate to the parent when in the filed and to trigger an alarm when it is stolen.


The entire system has a server to which it communicates. The server's scope is to offer ways of managing the parents and children on the filed, like: creating objectives, editing objectives, tracking lost children, etc. Also the server's scope is to offer ways to manage the users, monitor and manage the objectives and see detailed data about the entire system (logs, statiscics).
